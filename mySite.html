Description

Dodge asteroids to stay afloat in this retro rocket arcade game. Play the game by tapping either side of the screen to maneuver around falling asteroids. Hold either side of the screen to perform barrel rolls and hold both sides of the screen to brake and absorb impacts.

Reach score 100 to disable ads forever.
